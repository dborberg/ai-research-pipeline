<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s AI cycle looks less like a pure model race and more like a contest over infrastructure access, regulatory permission, and enterprise-grade control. Data center moratoria, bank-regulator scrutiny, and new agentic infrastructure benchmarks all point to the same direction: AI demand is real, but scaling it depends on power, cooling, governance, auditability, and cost efficiency. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key message is that the AI opportunity is broadening beyond the largest platforms into compliance, data center infrastructure, semiconductors, cooling, power, and workflow-control layers.</p>
