<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s AI news points to an investment cycle increasingly shaped by policy permission, infrastructure access, and capital intensity rather than model releases alone. Governments are moving to either accelerate AI infrastructure through land, power, and permits or slow deployment through labor, advertising, agent, and data center rules. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key message is that AI adoption is becoming a governed infrastructure buildout, with winners and risks likely to extend beyond mega-cap platforms into power, data centers, compliance, workflow software, and industrial automation.</p>
