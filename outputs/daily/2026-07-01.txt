<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s strongest signal is that AI adoption is becoming a governed infrastructure cycle, not just a model-performance cycle. Regulated enterprises are looking for sovereign cloud, auditability, and agent controls before moving AI into production workflows, while data center permitting, power systems, and inference economics are becoming gating issues for capacity growth. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key message is that the AI opportunity is broadening from frontier models to the enabling layers: cloud architecture, governance software, power equipment, cooling, semiconductors, robotics, and compliance infrastructure.</p>
