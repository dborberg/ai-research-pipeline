<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s strongest signal is that the AI investment cycle is moving further from model excitement into the harder work of infrastructure, governance, and enterprise deployment. The biggest developments point to three bottlenecks that increasingly determine adoption speed: power-connected data center sites, practical agent deployment stacks, and regulatory clarity. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the useful framing is that AI monetization is becoming a systems story, not just a software or semiconductor story. The read-through extends to data center financing, utilities, electrical equipment, cybersecurity, compliance tooling, industrial software, and firms that control enterprise workflow surfaces.</p>
