<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s strongest theme is that AI is becoming a production-scale infrastructure, governance, and financing story at the same time. Enterprise tools are moving from standalone copilots toward embedded agents inside major workflow platforms, while data-center growth is increasingly constrained by power availability, permitting, and local political scrutiny. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key point is that AI monetization is broadening beyond model providers and chips into utilities, grid equipment, compliance, automation, cloud workflow platforms, and financing structures tied to physical capacity.</p>
