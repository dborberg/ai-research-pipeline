<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>The dominant signal today is that AI is becoming a full-cycle investment theme, not just a model innovation story. The strongest developments point to three constraints shaping the next phase: capital-market discipline, power availability, and governance readiness. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the message is balanced: AI still has large productivity and infrastructure potential, but the market is starting to test whether spending, policy, and real-world deployment can convert excitement into durable returns. The investment discussion should broaden beyond the largest platforms toward credit conditions, grid capacity, compliance infrastructure, automation, and valuation discipline.</p>
