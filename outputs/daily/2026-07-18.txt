<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s AI tape shows the investment cycle broadening from model performance and mega-cap leadership into physical deployment, infrastructure constraints, and policy friction. Local data center moratoriums are becoming a practical bottleneck for AI capacity, while ASML’s outlook shows that AI demand is still translating into upstream semiconductor equipment needs. At the same time, Toyota’s expanded Nvidia partnership and robotics funding signals point to AI moving into vehicles, factories, construction, and healthcare workflows. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key message is that AI is no longer just a software story; it is increasingly a power, permitting, semicap, industrial automation, governance, and platform-distribution story.</p>
