<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s AI news points to an investment cycle that is becoming more physical, more governed, and more capital-intensive. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key message is that AI exposure increasingly extends beyond the largest platforms into power, semiconductors, data infrastructure, governance, industrial automation, and local policy risk.</p>
