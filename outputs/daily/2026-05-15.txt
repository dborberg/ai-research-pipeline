<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s strongest theme is that the AI investment cycle is moving deeper into physical-world constraints: local permitting, power availability, water use, memory supply, and community acceptance. The news flow suggests that AI is no longer just a model or software story; it is increasingly an infrastructure, regulation, and capital allocation story. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key takeaway is that AI exposure should be evaluated not only through platform companies, but also through the bottlenecks and second-derivative beneficiaries that determine whether AI capacity can actually be built and monetized.</p>
