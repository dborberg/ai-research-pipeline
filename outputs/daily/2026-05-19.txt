<p><strong>May 19, 2026</strong></p>

<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>The dominant signal today is that the AI cycle is increasingly constrained by physical infrastructure, power availability, permitting, and political tolerance rather than model capability alone. Large platform capex, data center financing, utility-price concerns, and local moratorium debates all point to AI becoming a real-assets and policy-access story. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key framing is that AI exposure now extends well beyond software and semiconductors into utilities, grid equipment, cooling, interconnect, compliance, and infrastructure finance.</p>
