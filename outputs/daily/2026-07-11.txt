<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s AI news points to an investment cycle that is increasingly constrained by real-world infrastructure, policy permission, and capital-market absorption. The strongest signal is that AI buildout is no longer just a software or model story; it is showing up in inflation commentary, chip and data center supply chains, local permitting battles, and sovereign access to compute. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key takeaway is that AI exposure should be monitored across platforms, infrastructure suppliers, power and grid constraints, governance tools, and financing conditions rather than only through the largest model companies.</p>
