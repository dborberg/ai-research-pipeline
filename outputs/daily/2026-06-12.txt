<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>The dominant signal today is that the AI cycle is becoming a physical infrastructure, financing, and governance story as much as a model-capability story. Amazon’s water disclosure and large AI data center financing show that compute growth now depends on balance sheets, local resources, permitting, and community acceptance. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the implication is to broaden AI discussions beyond mega-cap platforms to power, cooling, water, semiconductors, automation, compliance, and software business-model durability.</p>
