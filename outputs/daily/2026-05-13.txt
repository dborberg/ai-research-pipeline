<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>The day’s news reinforces that the AI investment cycle is increasingly constrained by infrastructure, power access, permitting, and policy rather than model capability alone. Data center financing, local moratoriums, photonics investment, and even exploratory space-based infrastructure all point to a more physical, capital-intensive phase of AI buildout. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key message is that AI exposure is broadening beyond the largest platforms into grid equipment, power systems, optical networking, industrial automation, compliance, and governance. The opportunity set is widening, but so are execution risks around regulation, margins, and community acceptance.</p>
