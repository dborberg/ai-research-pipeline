<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s strongest signal is that the AI cycle is becoming less about standalone models and more about the operating systems around them: power, permitting, governance, cost control, and production workflow integration. The same article set points to data center constraints, state-level AI rules, and enterprise use cases where AI is being embedded into research, energy-market oversight, and robotics workflows. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key message is that AI monetization increasingly depends on execution capacity beyond the model layer. The investment discussion should therefore include infrastructure, governance, industrial automation, healthcare robotics, and enterprise software controls alongside the largest AI platforms.</p>
