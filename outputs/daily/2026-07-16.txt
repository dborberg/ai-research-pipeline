<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>The dominant signal today is that the AI investment cycle is becoming as much about physical permission as technical capability. Data center moratoriums, political pushback, and policy forums show that power access, zoning, community acceptance, and strategic-infrastructure framing are now central to AI capacity growth. At the same time, Nvidia’s robotics partnerships and Anthropic’s enterprise signal show AI moving beyond chat interfaces into production workflows and physical-world automation. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key point is that AI exposure is broadening beyond model leaders into infrastructure, governance, power, industrial automation, and companies that can help enterprises deploy AI safely at scale.</p>
