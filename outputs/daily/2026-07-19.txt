<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s strongest signal is that the AI investment cycle is becoming less about model releases and more about access, governance, siting, power, and physical deployment. Regulators and local governments are increasingly shaping who can use data, where compute can be built, and what environmental costs must be addressed. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key message is that AI remains a growth theme, but the bottlenecks are moving into policy, infrastructure, and enterprise readiness. That favors portfolio monitoring beyond the largest platforms, including grid infrastructure, power systems, governance software, robotics, and compliance-heavy enterprise platforms.</p>
