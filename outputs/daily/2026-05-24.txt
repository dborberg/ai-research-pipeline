<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s strongest theme is that AI is increasingly constrained by physical-world bottlenecks rather than model capability alone. Local data center moratoriums, fiber capacity concerns, and infrastructure asset transactions all point to a market where compute demand is colliding with zoning, power, cooling, connectivity, and community acceptance. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key message is that the AI cycle is broadening beyond mega-cap platforms into real estate, grid equipment, networking, cooling, permitting, and local policy risk. The investment discussion should increasingly focus on who can scale AI infrastructure reliably, not just who has the best model.</p>
