<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s AI cycle looks increasingly defined by the handoff from model enthusiasm to real-world deployment constraints. Enterprise platforms are embedding agentic AI into commerce and workflow systems, while data center growth is running into local permitting, labor, grid, and energy-storage questions. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key point is that AI monetization now depends on production readiness, power availability, governance, and infrastructure execution, not just better models.</p>
