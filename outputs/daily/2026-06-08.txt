<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>The day’s strongest theme is that AI’s next constraint is increasingly physical, political and operational rather than purely model-based. Local moratoriums, data center construction strategies, fiber buildouts, electricity sourcing and governed enterprise deployment all point to AI moving from experimentation into infrastructure-dependent production. For CPM, advisor and wholesaler conversations, the key takeaway is that the AI investment cycle is broadening beyond frontier models and GPUs into permitting, grid capacity, connectivity, compliance, workflow governance and physical automation.</p>
