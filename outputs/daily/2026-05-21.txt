<h2>Beyond the Horizon: Daily Riffs from the Gen AI Songbook</h2>
<p><strong>May 21, 2026</strong></p>

<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>The AI investment cycle is increasingly being defined by physical constraints, not just model capability. Local data center moratoriums, on-site power partnerships, semiconductor investment, and advanced cooling needs all point to the same issue: AI demand is scaling faster than permitting, grid capacity, thermal management, and supply chains can comfortably absorb. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the useful framing is that AI is becoming a real-asset, infrastructure, and policy story alongside a software and semiconductor story.</p>
