<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s strongest theme is that AI is becoming a physical, regulated, and systems-level investment cycle rather than a model-only story. Power costs, local permitting, semiconductor integration, and government workflow adoption all point to AI moving into the infrastructure and policy layers that determine scale. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key message is that AI exposure increasingly extends beyond mega-cap platforms into utilities, grid infrastructure, advanced semiconductors, compliance software, intellectual property workflows, and data center supply chains. The adoption story is also becoming more institutional: enterprises and public agencies are emphasizing governance, auditability, security, and workflow integration before scaling AI into production.</p>
