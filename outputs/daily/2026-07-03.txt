<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>The strongest signal today is that the AI investment cycle is becoming less about chatbots alone and more about buildable, governed, power-constrained infrastructure. Data center projects, federal freeze proposals, and behind-the-meter power rules show that physical capacity and policy permission are now central to AI scaling. At the same time, funding and deployment activity in embodied AI, life sciences agents, and sovereign AI point to AI moving into more specialized enterprise workflows. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key message is that AI opportunity is broadening, but execution risk is rising across power, permitting, governance, and capital intensity.</p>
