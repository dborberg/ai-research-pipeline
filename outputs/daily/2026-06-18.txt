<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s strongest theme is that the AI investment cycle is becoming less about model announcements alone and more about the real-world operating system needed to deploy AI at scale. Enterprise platforms are adding governance, grounding, and agent infrastructure, while banks, lawyers, universities, and local governments are defining the guardrails for production use. At the same time, data center siting, grid access, cooling, and regional policy are emerging as practical bottlenecks that can shape capex timing and second-derivative beneficiaries. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key message is that AI exposure increasingly spans cloud platforms, cybersecurity, compliance, power infrastructure, cooling, robotics, and local permitting risk.</p>
