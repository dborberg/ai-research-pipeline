<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>The strongest signal today is that AI is moving deeper into the real economy, where financing, governance, local permitting, and workflow controls are becoming as important as model performance. The investment cycle is broadening from chips and chatbots toward debt-funded infrastructure, agent orchestration, compliance frameworks, and data-center siting constraints. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key point is that AI adoption is increasingly a systems story: capital markets fund the buildout, governments define access rules, enterprises demand control layers, and local communities can slow deployment.</p>
