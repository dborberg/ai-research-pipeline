<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s AI news reinforces that the investment cycle is moving beyond model excitement into execution risk: compute access, power availability, state regulation, enterprise controls, and capital intensity are becoming the gating factors. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key message is that AI exposure is broadening into semiconductors, data centers, grid infrastructure, compliance, and workflow software, but the dispersion between durable beneficiaries and crowded narratives may widen.</p>
