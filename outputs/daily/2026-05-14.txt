<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>The AI investment cycle is increasingly becoming a real-world execution story, not just a model-performance story. Today’s news points to three practical constraints shaping the next phase: local resistance to data center buildouts, companies reallocating labor budgets toward AI, and enterprise governance lagging employee usage. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key message is that AI opportunity is broadening beyond mega-cap platforms into infrastructure, compliance, automation, power, and workflow redesign, while also creating new permitting, policy, and implementation risks.</p>
