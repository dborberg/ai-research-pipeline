<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>The day’s strongest signal is that the AI investment cycle is becoming less about model announcements and more about the real-world systems required to deploy AI at scale. Local permitting, power availability, cooling, chip architecture, security, and governance are now shaping how quickly enterprise AI can move from pilots into production. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key message is that AI exposure is broadening beyond the largest platforms into infrastructure suppliers, compliance layers, workflow software, and physical automation enablers. The market is still tolerating elevated AI capex, but the next phase will likely reward evidence of efficiency, utilization, and durable enterprise demand.</p>
