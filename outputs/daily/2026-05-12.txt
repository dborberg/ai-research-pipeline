<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>The AI investment cycle is increasingly being shaped by physical constraints, policy friction, and financing capacity rather than model launches alone. Today’s stories show federal efforts to speed data center permitting running directly into local moratoriums, community resistance, and power-system funding needs. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key point is that AI remains a growth theme, but the investable opportunity set is broadening into power, permitting, memory, compliance, and infrastructure execution. Portfolio monitoring should focus not only on the largest platforms, but also on the second-derivative companies and policy bottlenecks that determine whether AI capacity can actually be built.</p>
