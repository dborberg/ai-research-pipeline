<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>The AI investment cycle is increasingly being shaped by the physical and political limits of deployment, not just model capability. Today’s strongest signals show data centers becoming a financing, permitting, public-acceptance, and geopolitical issue, while enterprise adoption is moving toward governed agents that require better data foundations and compliance controls. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key framing is that AI upside is broadening beyond model owners into infrastructure finance, power and permitting exposure, governance software, data platforms, robotics, and second-derivative industrial beneficiaries.</p>
