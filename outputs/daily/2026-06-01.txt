<h3>TOP THEME OF THE DAY</h3>
<p>Today’s strongest theme is that the AI cycle is becoming a physical infrastructure, governance, and policy execution story, not just a model-capability story. Large data center commitments, semiconductor export strength, and rack-scale supply chain buildouts point to durable demand for compute, power, networking, cooling, and manufacturing capacity. At the same time, local moratoriums and sector-specific governance rules show that adoption will increasingly depend on permissioning, auditability, and community acceptance. For CPM, advisor, and wholesaler conversations, the key message is that AI exposure is broadening beyond the largest platforms into infrastructure suppliers, compliance layers, industrial automation, and policy-sensitive deployment bottlenecks.</p>
