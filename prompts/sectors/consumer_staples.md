## Sector Adapter: Consumer Staples

- Sector definition: Food, beverage, household products, personal care, packaged goods, tobacco, and related distribution ecosystems.
- Main business models: Branded product sales, private label, distributor relationships, category management, recurring consumption, promotional trade spending.
- Value chain notes: Brand building, formulation, procurement, manufacturing, logistics, shelf placement, retail negotiation, trade promotion, and consumer insights shape sector economics.
- Key revenue drivers: Volume, pricing, mix, distribution breadth, category share, promotional effectiveness, innovation success, retailer relationships.
- Key cost centers: Raw materials, packaging, manufacturing, logistics, trade spend, advertising, salesforce and category management.
- High-probability Gen AI use cases: Marketing content localization, consumer insight synthesis, trade promotion planning support, sales enablement, customer service, product concept testing, procurement and documentation workflows.
- Adoption bottlenecks: Retailer power, limited tolerance for brand mistakes, fragmented data between manufacturer and retailer, slower cycle times, modest willingness to pay for AI-heavy experiences by end consumers.
- Likely beneficiaries: Global brands with rich consumer and retail data, retailers and distributors using AI for category planning, software and services vendors improving marketing productivity.
- Incumbents at risk: Admin-heavy organizations, smaller brands without data scale, agencies or service layers exposed to content automation, low-margin intermediaries with limited differentiation.
- Important KPIs or signals investors should watch: SG&A leverage, trade spend efficiency, innovation hit rate, gross margin, forecast accuracy, fill rates, promotional ROI, retailer shelf share, working capital turns.
- Sector-specific analytical traps to avoid: Do not overstate direct revenue impact where products are physical and mature; do not ignore retailer bargaining power; do not confuse cheaper content creation with durable brand advantage.
